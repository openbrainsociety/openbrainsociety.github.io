What is the Problem?

We live in a world where most of the students are still restricted only to academics books, which undermines the child's inherent ability to explore resulting in a confused state while making decisions. They lack the motivation,guidence and platform to explore further.

We are here to solve



Learning to Learn using Real Life Relatable Examples

Students' willingness in the first place and followed by enthusiasm to learn multiplies when they relate academic concepts to the real world scenarios.

Prime Focus on Lateral Thinking and Decision Making Abilities

We encourage students to come up with their own unique approaches even when the solution may not be the correct one.

Basic Maths and Logical Reasoning

Maths and Logic go hand in hand. These are essential at every stage of life. We help students in discovering their hidden scientists by making them face new problems.

Activity Based Group Learning

Our activities include Public Speaking, Group Quizzes, Motivational Sessions and many more which are important ingredients for the All Round Development.

How It Works

Tieing up with Schools

We believe in working together and that is why, we tie up with schools as academic partners to deliver our program as an Add-on.
Contact us to get to your schools this differentiating program

Capability Based Modules

We divided the modules into Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced to match the pace of the students. A student is promoted to next module when he/she completes the one they are in.

FIVE Months for each Module

Our supporting faculty helps students for whole duration(approx 4hrs/week for 5 months) of each module. after which the students will be ready to take the next module

Monthly Tests and Activities

Monthly tests and activities are conducted to enhance progress of students
